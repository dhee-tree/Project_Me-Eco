About Us

Project Me-Eco is an innovative, interdisciplinary and intergenerational solution, that focuses on mental health and environmental crisis.

Our aim is to empower people to reflect, heal and grow.

Project Me-Eco is a collaboration between the University of Derby, Derby CAN, The Woodland Trust, and the local community.

Our project is funded by the Arts Council England, Paul Hamlyn Foundation and the National Lottery.
